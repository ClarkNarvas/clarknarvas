What do Vampires eat?





Mythical Vampires






Mythical Vampires live only on blood and do not eat human food
though sometimes they can sit with their human companion while they're eating.






Real Vampires (Sanguinarian Vampires)






These vampires do require some amounts of blood for their survival but
having it only will not keep them alive. They aren't undead, they just have a disease
which makes blood vital for their survival.






Psi-Vampires





Like Real Vampires, these vampires aren't undead.
To survive, they need to sip on small amounts of energy from others and eat food.

How to kill a Vampire?

Sunlight

The easiest way to kill a Vampire is by exposing them to direct sunlight for 10 seconds,
While it's one of the most easiest ways you'd need to plan it because
Vampires aren't stupid, and they're very and and strong. They know if they go
into sunlight they'll burn so try setting up a trap.

Wooden Stake

To kill a vampire using a wooden stake is to strike first, Vampires aren't very good
at defending themselves and they can let their guard down easily.

Using Silver as a way to slow down
the healing

You cannot directly use silver as a weapon, you can use it to slow the healing process down.
To actually kill the Vampire with it you'd need a lot of it.
